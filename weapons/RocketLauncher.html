The Axe is the starting weapon that is always be available to use.



The Rocket Launcher is the most iconic weapon from quake, due to it's high damage and movement potential.

The Rocket Launcher deals 100-120 direct impact damage, and 40-120 damage from splash damage.
If Quad Damage is obtained, it can insta-gib most enemies. It can even make quick work
of Vores. Just remember that Shamblers take half damage from explosives.
Be careful while rocket jumping, all of your self-splash damge is multiplied while using quad damage.

Rottweilers are the rarest enemy in the game, and one of the easiest to dispatch.




Rottweilers have very low health, the same as a rotfish and can be easily put down with 1-2 shotgun blasts,
they can often be found around Grunts and Enforcers. Rottweilers are first found on E1M1: The Slipgate Complex.
Their bite attack can deal 1-24 damage, while their jump attack deals 10-20. The rotwreiler is an easy enemy
to avoid due to it only having a melee attack. interestingly enough, the rotweiler only appears in 4 levels.

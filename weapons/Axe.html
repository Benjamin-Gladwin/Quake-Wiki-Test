The Axe is the starting weapon that is always be available to use.



The Axe is arguably the least useful weapon in the game due to it's lacking damage and range.

The Axe deals 20 damage, which is enough to take on low tier enemies such as Grunts.
If Quad Damage is obtained, it can insta-gib most low tier enemies. (which is useful against zombies)
It can even make quick work of Shamblers.
